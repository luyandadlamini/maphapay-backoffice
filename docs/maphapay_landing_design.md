## VISUAL RULES

- Use real people
- Avoid stock feel
- Use whitespace heavily
- Keep layouts clean

---

## DESIGN RULES

- One message per section
- One action per section
- Keep everything simple

---

## MOTION

- Subtle animations
- Smooth transitions
- Light interaction feedback

---

## CLAUDE PROMPT

Design a premium fintech landing page for MaphaPay, a mobile wallet built for Eswatini. Focus on lifestyle storytelling with real people using money in everyday situations. Combine clean, minimal design with bold mobile-first phone mockups. Use blue and gold sparingly, with strong whitespace. The tone should be simple, human, and confident. Avoid technical language. Make the experience feel modern, trusted, and culturally relevant.

---

## FINAL CHECK

- Does this feel like real life?
- Is it simple enough?
- Does it build trust immediately?
- Does it feel premium?
